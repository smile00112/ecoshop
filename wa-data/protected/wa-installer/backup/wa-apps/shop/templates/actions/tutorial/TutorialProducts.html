Add products to sell

Click Products → New product to create your first product.

Once a product has been added, view it in your storefront and try placing a test order.

Import products in bulk

Shop-Script supports many ways of adding multiple products at once.

  • Bulk image upload: Drag & drop a set of images from your computer directly into Shop-Script and create multiple products at once.
  • Import product from a CSV file: Your CSV file (you can create it using Microsoft Excel, Open Office, iWork, or similar apps) will be processed line by line while adding new products and categories as fast as a only computer can do it.
  • Migrate to Shop-Script: Transfer data from third-party ecommerce platforms to Shop-Script.
