Choose design

Proceed to Storefronts → Themes to browse available design themes and pick your favorite.

Try installing some of the free themes. All themes are downloaded from Webasyst Store and are automatically installed in your online store.

Customize

Once a theme has been installed, customize its design using the built-in Appearance and Templates tools.

Theme files are saved into a special data folder on the server (wa-data/public/) to ensure that all your changes in the template files are not lost when you install theme updates.
